The tests were conducted based on the following caveats:
* All foot traffic have tried the demo
* Each unique customer can purchase at most 1 product
* Bellevue and Tyson stores are equivalent in sales unit, sales price, and foot traffic

t test: Independent sample t-test: control_during=treatment_during ?
* Compare two random samples (control and treatment) during the campaign. Assume two samples are equivalent.
* If difference is found, it is only due to campaign effects
